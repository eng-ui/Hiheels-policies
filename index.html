Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

About the App

Hi Heels is an internal business management application used exclusively by approved team members to manage inventory, orders, and related business operations.

Data We Collect

We collect your email address solely for authentication via Google Sign-In. No other personal information is collected beyond what is strictly required for account access.

Business Data

Business records entered into the app — including products, orders, and inventory entries — are stored securely using Google Firebase and Google Sheets, and used only for the operation of the business.

🔒

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. All data transmitted by the app is encrypted in transit.
