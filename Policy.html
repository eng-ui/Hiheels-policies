Hi Heels App

This page contains the Privacy Policy and Account Deletion information for the Hi Heels app.

Privacy Policy

Hi Heels is an internal business management application used by approved team members to manage inventory, orders, and related business operations.

We collect your email address for the purpose of authentication via Google Sign-In. We do not collect any other personal information beyond what is required for account authentication.

Business data entered into the app (such as products, orders, and inventory records) is stored securely using Google Firebase and Google Sheets services, and is used solely for the operation of the business.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

All data transmitted by the app is encrypted in transit.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Qamarmuhammadwaleed@gmail.com.

Account Deletion

Hi Heels is an internal business management app used only by approved team members. To request that your account access be removed, contact us at Qamarmuhammadwaleed@gmail.com with the subject "Account Removal Request."

Upon request, your account will be blocked from accessing the app. Business records associated with your account (such as orders and inventory entries) are retained as part of the company's operational and financial records, since these records belong to the business rather than to an individual user.
